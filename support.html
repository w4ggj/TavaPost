Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my Instagram account failing to connect?
Ensure that your Instagram account is a Professional or Creator account, and that it is properly linked to a Facebook Page you manage. The API requires this bridge to authenticate successfully.
How long does image analysis take?
Our engine downscales your image and processes it through the visual model. This typically takes between 15 and 45 seconds depending on server load and image complexity.
Can I upgrade or cancel my subscription?
Yes. You can manage your subscription tier, update payment methods, or cancel at any time directly through the billing portal in your dashboard.
