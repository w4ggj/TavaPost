Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 26, 2026

Welcome to TavaOne Studio. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you visit our website and tell you about your privacy rights.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to our users. This includes:

  • Account Information: Email addresses and secure passwords used for authentication.
  • Connected Accounts: When you connect social media profiles (e.g., Facebook, Instagram), we store the necessary authorization tokens to publish content on your behalf via our partner integrations (e.g., Zernio).
  • User Content: Images, text prompts, and generated drafts that you upload or create within the Studio.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect strictly to operate, maintain, and provide the features of the Studio. This includes utilizing AI integrations to analyze your uploaded media and generate caption variations, as well as pushing your finalized content to your selected social platforms.

3. Third-Party Services

We utilize third-party services to facilitate our operations. Your data may be shared securely with:

  • Authentication & Storage: Supabase for secure user management and image hosting.
  • Publishing Engine: Zernio and Meta (Facebook/Instagram) to route and publish your content.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

You can request the deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting our support team. Upon request, we will sever all connections to your social profiles and delete your uploaded assets from our active servers.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@tavaone.com.
